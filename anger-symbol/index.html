💢 Anger Symbol

A red symbol found in Anime/Manga and used to represent the veins popping on a very angry person.

Also used in other comic books to indicate a punch landed, perhaps next to the words “Bam” or “Pow”.

Anger Symbol was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
