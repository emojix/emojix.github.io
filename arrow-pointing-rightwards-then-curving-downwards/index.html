⤵️ Right Arrow Curving Down

A curved arrow pointing down.

Right Arrow Curving Down was approved as part of Unicode 3.2 in 2002 under the name “Arrow Pointing Rightwards Then Curving Downwards” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
