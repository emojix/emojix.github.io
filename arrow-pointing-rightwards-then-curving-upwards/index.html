⤴️ Right Arrow Curving Up

A curved arrow pointing up.

Right Arrow Curving Up was approved as part of Unicode 3.2 in 2002 under the name “Arrow Pointing Rightwards Then Curving Upwards” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
