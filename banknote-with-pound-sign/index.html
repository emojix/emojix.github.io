💷 Pound Banknote

British money – a £20 note, used in England, Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland. Colloquially referred to as “quid” in parts of the United Kingdom.

Pound Banknote was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Banknote With Pound Sign” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
