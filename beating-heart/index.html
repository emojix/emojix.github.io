💓 Beating Heart

A heart with vibration / movement lines above it, indicating it is a beating heart.

This emoji is used as a beating heart either representing life, or love.

Beating Heart was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
