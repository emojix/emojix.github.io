♥️ Heart Suit

A heart symbol emoji, which is used in card games for the hearts suit. Generally shown in red, despite the name. Not to be confused with the black heart emoji.

See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Heart Suit was approved as part of Unicode 1.1 in 1993 under the name “Black Heart Suit” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
