◀️ Reverse Button

A triangle-shaped arrow, pointing left.

See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Reverse Button was approved as part of Unicode 1.1 in 1993 under the name “Black Left-Pointing Triangle” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
