▶️ Play Button

A triangle-shaped arrow, pointing right. Commonly used as a symbol for play.

See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Play Button was approved as part of Unicode 1.1 in 1993 under the name “Black Right-Pointing Triangle” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
