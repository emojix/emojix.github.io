➡️ Right Arrow

An arrow, pointing right.

See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Right Arrow was approved as part of Unicode 1.1 in 1993 under the name “Black Rightwards Arrow” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
