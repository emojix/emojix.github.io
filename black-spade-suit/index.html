♠️ Spade Suit

A spade symbol emoji, which is used in card games for the spades suit.

See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Spade Suit was approved as part of Unicode 1.1 in 1993 under the name “Black Spade Suit” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
