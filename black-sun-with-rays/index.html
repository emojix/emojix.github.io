☀️ Sun

The sun; a warm ball of fire that keeps life on earth viable. Represents sunny weather, or hot days.

See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Sun was approved as part of Unicode 1.1 in 1993 under the name “Black Sun With Rays” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
