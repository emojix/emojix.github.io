☎️ Telephone

A traditional landline (home phone) telephone with a receiver on top of the base, where numbers are dialled.

See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Telephone was approved as part of Unicode 1.1 in 1993 under the name “Black Telephone” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
