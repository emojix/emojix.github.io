🚏 Bus Stop

A sign indicating the location of a bus stop by the side of a road.

Some platforms display this as a red, white and blue sign which can be found in parts of Japan. This interpretation has a slight resemblance to the Pepsi Globe.

Other platforms base this emoji design on bus stop signage of other regions.

Bus Stop was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
