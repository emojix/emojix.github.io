💹 Chart Increasing With Yen

A graph showing the current value of the Japanese Yen.

Chart Increasing With Yen was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Chart With Upwards Trend and Yen Sign” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
