📈 Chart Increasing

A chart showing figures increasing over time, with an upward tick. A type of line graph.

Chart Increasing was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Chart With Upwards Trend” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
