📫 Closed Mailbox With Raised Flag

A closed mailbox, with the flag raised.

The flag on the side of the mailbox is used in the USA to indicate mail has been delivered, or that mail is in the box to be picked up by the postal worker.

Closed Mailbox With Raised Flag was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
