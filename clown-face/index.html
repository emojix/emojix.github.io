🤡 Clown Face

A face with distinctively colored face makeup intended to entertain children with physical comedy. May also juggle, or create  balloon animals. Some people consider clowns to be terrifying.

Clown Face was approved as part of Unicode 9.0 in 2016 and added to Emoji 3.0 in 2016.
