Part displeased, part unsure what to think. This face isn’t sad, but is a bit confused and puzzled by the situation.

On the spectrum of displeasure, confused face sits between neutral face and unamused face. The single squinting eye in Google’s Android artwork is more effective at conveying confusion than the Apple artwork.

Confused Face was approved as part of Unicode 6.1 in 2012 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
