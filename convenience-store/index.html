🏪 Convenience Store

A shop that is generally open for longer hours than a regular supermarket, sometimes 24 hours per day.

Sells a range of goods from snacks, meals, hygiene products and household items.

Convenience Store was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
