💳 Credit Card

A credit card emoji, showing the back side of the card with magnetic strip.

The Apple artwork for this symbol shows an authorised signature for John Appleseed, with 448 as the security code.

Credit Card was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
