🚯 No Littering

A person dropping litter (rubbish, trash) with a red stroke through it, indicating that littering is prohibited in this area.

Usually implied by the fact that littering is not welcome anywhere, this sign serves as a reminder in areas that are known to attract litter.

No Littering was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Do Not Litter Symbol” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
