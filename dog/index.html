🐕 Dog

A dog, which appears as various different breeds depending on the platform it is being viewed on.

This emoji traditionally displayed an Akita Inu or Japanese Akita: a breed of dog which originates from the northern part of Japan, and is sometimes used to represent the dog in the Doge meme.

One of the twelve animals of the Chinese Zodiac.

Dog was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
