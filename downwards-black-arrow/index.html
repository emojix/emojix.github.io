⬇️ Down Arrow

An arrow, pointing down.

See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Down Arrow was approved as part of Unicode 4.0 in 2003 under the name “Downwards Black Arrow” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
