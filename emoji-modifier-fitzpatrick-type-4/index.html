🏽 Medium Skin Tone

A moderate brown skin tone modifier, which can be applied to a range of human emoji characters.

Type 4 on the Fitzpatrick Scale of skin tones is described as: Moderate brown; typical Mediterranean skin tone — Rarely burns, always tans well

Medium Skin Tone was approved as part of Unicode 8.0 in 2015 under the name “Emoji Modifier Fitzpatrick Type-4” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
