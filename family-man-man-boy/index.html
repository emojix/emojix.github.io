👨‍👨‍👦 Family: Man, Man, Boy

A family with two fathers, and one son.

The Family: Man, Man, Boy emoji is a sequence of the 👨 Man, 👨 Man and 👦 Boy emojis. These are combined using a zero width joiner between each character and display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Family: Man, Man, Boy was added to Emoji 2.0 in 2015.
