🎅 Santa Claus

Santa Claus, known in some countries as Father Christmas, brings joy to children at Christmas time. Married to Mrs Claus.

The Santa emoji had a frown on Android 4.4, which was resolved in Android 5.0 after a bug report was filed. Father Christmas smiles in the current version of Android.

Note: Father Christmas has a different origin to Santa Claus. Both are represented by this single emoji.

Santa Claus was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Father Christmas” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

Modifiers

The Father Christmas emoji supports skin tone modifiers. A yellow (or other non-human) skin tone should be shown by default, unless an emoji modifier is applied.

  • 🎅🏻 Santa Claus: Light Skin Tone
  • 🎅🏼 Santa Claus: Medium-Light Skin Tone
  • 🎅🏽 Santa Claus: Medium Skin Tone
  • 🎅🏾 Santa Claus: Medium-Dark Skin Tone
  • 🎅🏿 Santa Claus: Dark Skin Tone

Platforms without support for emoji modifiers display a missing symbol character (box with a cross, alien, or question mark in it) next to any modified emoji.
