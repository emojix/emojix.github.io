🎆 Fireworks

Firework emoji, showing a explosion of colored light in the night sky, used for any number of celebrations such as New Year's Eve.

Colors vary by platform, and may include pink, purple, yellow, blue, and green.

Fireworks was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
