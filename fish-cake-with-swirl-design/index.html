🍥 Fish Cake With Swirl

A fishcake (or fish cake) that is used in some Asian meals, known as Narutomaki in Japanese. Each slice includes a spiral design for visual flair.

Fish Cake With Swirl was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Fish Cake With Swirl Design” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
