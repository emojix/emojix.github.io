🇨🇷 Costa Rica

The flag for Costa Rica, which may show as the letters CR on some platforms.

The Costa Rica emoji is a sequence of the 🇨 Regional Indicator Symbol Letter C and 🇷 Regional Indicator Symbol Letter R emojis. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Costa Rica was added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
