🇹🇦 Tristan Da Cunha

The flag for Tristan da Cunha, which may show as the letters TA on some platforms. This same flag is used for Ascension Island and St. Helena.

The Tristan Da Cunha emoji is a sequence of the 🇹 Regional Indicator Symbol Letter T and 🇦 Regional Indicator Symbol Letter A emojis. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Tristan Da Cunha was added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
