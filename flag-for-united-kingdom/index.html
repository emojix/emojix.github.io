🇬🇧 United Kingdom

The flag for the United Kingdom of Great Britain, which may show as the letters GB on some platforms.

The emoji flag list is based on ISO 3166-1 alpha-2: a list of internationally recognized two-letter country codes. For the purposes of ISO 3166-1, England, Scotland and Wales do not have their own emoji flags, and are represented by the Flag For United Kingdom.

The United Kingdom emoji is a sequence of the 🇬 Regional Indicator Symbol Letter G and 🇧 Regional Indicator Symbol Letter B emojis. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

United Kingdom was added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
