🇺🇸 United States

The flag for the United States of America (USA), which may show as the letters US on some platforms.

The United States emoji is a sequence of the 🇺 Regional Indicator Symbol Letter U and 🇸 Regional Indicator Symbol Letter S emojis. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

United States was added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
