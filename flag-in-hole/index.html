⛳ Flag in Hole

A red, triangular flag shown sitting in the hole of a golf course green. Some versions of this emoji also include golf balls.

Android previously displayed this emoji as the head of a putter (golf club) hitting a golf ball.

Not to be confused with the Triangular Flag on Post emoji.

Flag in Hole was approved as part of Unicode 5.2 in 2009 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
