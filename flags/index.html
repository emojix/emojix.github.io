🎌 Flags

List of emojis for country flags. 🇯🇵 🇰🇷 🇩🇪 🇨🇳 🇺🇸 🇫🇷 🇪🇸 🇮🇹 🇷🇺 🇬🇧 Emoji flags are supported on most major platforms, except Windows which displays two-letter country codes instead of emoji flag images.

The emoji flag list is based on ISO 3166-1: a list of internationally recognized two-letter country codes. For the purposes of ISO 3166-1, England, Scotland and Wales do not have their own emoji flags, and are represented by the Flag For United Kingdom. These flags have been approved for a future release.
