😦 Frowning Face With Open Mouth

Face with open eyes and open mouth. The Apple artwork for this emoji looks like a person yawning. Google and Microsoft’s artwork appear closer to the frowning description.

Frowning Face With Open Mouth was approved as part of Unicode 6.1 in 2012 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
