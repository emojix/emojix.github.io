🌐 Globe With Meridians

A symbol of planet earth showing lines of longitude and latitude. Commonly used as an icon for internationalization, and features on the iOS software keyboard to change keyboard languages.

Sometimes used as a representation of the World Wide Web.

Globe With Meridians was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
