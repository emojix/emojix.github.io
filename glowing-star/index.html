A five-point star of pale-yellow color, with small bursts of light shining around it. Generally used for visual flair rather than astronomical purposes.

On Snapchat, this emoji shows next to friends who have had their snaps replayed in the past 24 hours.

Glowing Star was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
