ğŸ Goat

A goat, displayed in white, gray, or brown depending on platform.  Previously appeared as a Saanen goat on iOS, but changed to a Toggenberg goat in recent versions.

Can be used in relation to the phrase Greatest Of All Time (GOAT). May be confused with the ram emoji at small sizes.

Goat was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
