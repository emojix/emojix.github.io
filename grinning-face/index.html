😀 Grinning Face

A face with a big open (grinning) mouth, showing teeth. Differs only slightly from the Smiling Face With Open Mouth And Smiling Eyes by the fact that these eyes are small circles, instead of the emoji-style smiling eyes.

Grinning Face was approved as part of Unicode 6.1 in 2012 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
