💂 Guard

A British guardsman, known as a foot guard. Wears a tall black hat with a yellow chin-strap. Must look straight ahead at all times, even when tourists attempt to distract them.

Guard was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Guardsman” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
