🐹 Hamster Face

A cute hamster, with pink ears, orange over half its face, and white/gray for the other part of the face. Has three whiskers on each side.

Hamster Face was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
