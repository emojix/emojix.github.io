❤️ Red Heart

A classic love heart emoji, used for expressions of love. Displayed in various shades of red on most platforms. A similar emoji exists for the heart suit in a deck of playing cards.

On Snapchat, this emoji displays next to a friend when you have been #1 BFs with each other for two consecutive weeks.

When used in WhatsApp, this heart emoji animates, showing a beating animation where the heart gets larger and smaller.

Note: the Unicode character name “Heavy Black Heart” pre-dates color emoji, when the term referred to a solid, black ❤︎ heart character. See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Red Heart was approved as part of Unicode 1.1 in 1993 under the name “Heavy Black Heart” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
