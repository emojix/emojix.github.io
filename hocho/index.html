🔪 Kitchen Knife

A versatile type of knife commonly found in kitchens in Japan. Generally used for cutting and filleting meat and fish.

Not to be confused with the dagger knife (weapon).

Kitchen Knife was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Hocho” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
