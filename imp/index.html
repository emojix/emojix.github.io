👿 Angry Face With Horns

A less-evil version of a demon; the Imp is a cheeky and mischievous character originating in Germanic Folkore.

Shows as purple on iOS, orange on Android, and red on Windows.

Angry Face With Horns was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Imp” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
