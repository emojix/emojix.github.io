🏣 Japanese Post Office

A Japanese post office, featuring the テ Japanese postal mark on the front of the building (or showing this mark instead of the building in Microsoft’s artwork).

Japanese Post Office was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
