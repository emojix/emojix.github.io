🥝 Kiwi Fruit

The kiwifruit emoji (also known as a Chinese gooseberry) is shown with brown skin and sliced open to show green flesh with black seeds on the inside

Kiwi Fruit was approved as part of Unicode 9.0 in 2016 under the name “Kiwifruit” and added to Emoji 3.0 in 2016.
