⬅️ Left Arrow

An arrow, pointing left.

See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Left Arrow was approved as part of Unicode 4.0 in 2003 under the name “Leftwards Black Arrow” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
