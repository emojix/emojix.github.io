🍋 Lemon

A yellow-colored fruit, known for its sour taste. Shown with one or two green leaves on some platforms.

Has been used in relation to the Beyoncé album, Lemonade.

Lemon was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
