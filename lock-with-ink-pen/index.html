🔏 Locked With Pen

A locked (closed) padlock, with an ink pen (otherwise known as a fountain pen) next to it. May related to key signing, a term used in digital security.

Locked With Pen was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Lock With Ink Pen” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
