🍭 Lollipop

A lollipop: circular candy on a stick.

Shown with a variety of colors (rainbow on Apple devices, yellow and pink for Android, pink on Windows, and red/yellow on Twitter), with a swirl design.

Can also be used in relation to Android 5.0 Lollipop.

Lollipop was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
