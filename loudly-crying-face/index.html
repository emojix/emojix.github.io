😭 Loudly Crying Face

A sad face with tears streaming down both cheeks. This face is distraught and inconsolable. Not to be confused with the tears of joy emoji.

Loudly Crying Face was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
