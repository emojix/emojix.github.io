🔅 Dim Button

An icon of a outlined sun, used when decreasing the brightness of a screen. Appears smaller than the High Brightness Symbol.

Dim Button was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Low Brightness Symbol” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
