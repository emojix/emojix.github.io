🀄 Mahjong Red Dragon

The red dragon tile in the game of Japanese Mahjong. White, with red 中 character printed on it.

Mahjong Red Dragon was approved as part of Unicode 5.1 in 2008 under the name “Mahjong Tile Red Dragon” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
