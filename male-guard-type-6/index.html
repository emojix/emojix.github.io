💂🏿‍♂️ Man Guard: Dark Skin Tone

The Man Guard: Dark Skin Tone emoji is a sequence of the 💂 Guardsman, 🏿 Emoji Modifier Fitzpatrick Type-6 and ♂ Male Sign emojis. These are combined using a zero width joiner between each character and display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Man Guard: Dark Skin Tone was added to Emoji 4.0 in 2016.
