👲 Man With Chinese Cap

A man wearing a gua pi mao; a type of Chinese hat. Sometimes referred to as a skullcap.

Man With Chinese Cap was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Man With Gua Pi Mao” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
