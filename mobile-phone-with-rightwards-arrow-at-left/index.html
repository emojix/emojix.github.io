📲 Mobile Phone With Arrow

A mobile phone, with an arrow pointing toward it. Intended to be used for an incoming phone call, but could also indicate anything on the device itself.

Mobile Phone With Arrow was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Mobile Phone With Rightwards Arrow at Left” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
