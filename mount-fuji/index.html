🗻 Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji (called Fuji-san in Japan) is the largest mountain in Japan. Often seen with snow on the peak in colder months of the year.

It is considered lucky in Japan to dream of Mt Fuji in the first dream of the new year.

Mount Fuji was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
