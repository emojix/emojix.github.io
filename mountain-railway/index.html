🚞 Mountain Railway

A train and railway in the mountains. Displayed as a funicular in Microsoft’s artwork, or a regular train on other platforms.

Android displays this train front-facing, leaving a tunnel, which appears similarly to the metro emoji at small sizes.

Mountain Railway was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
