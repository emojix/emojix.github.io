🎵 Musical Note

A music note emoji, which can denote song lyrics, or other music related topics.

Displays as two eighth-notes (quavers in British English) connected with a beam in the Apple artwork. A single eight-note (quaver) is used in Microsoft and Google’s emoji font.

Musical Note was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
