The number eighteen with a red stroke through it, indicating a location where a person must be aged 18 or above to enter.

The Samsung version of this emoji displays the number 19 instead of 18. This may be due to the practice of counting the first year of life as one (not zero) in parts of East Asia such as South Korea (the location of the Samsung Headquarters).
