🔩 Nut and Bolt

A nut shown with a bolt (or screw), usually made of steel and shown with a silver/gray color on most platforms. The angle of the nut and bolt varies by vendor, but is most commonly displayed diagonally with the tip in the bottom right corner.

Appears similarly to a dumbbell on Android, and previously displayed a screw and bolt on iOS.

Nut and Bolt was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
