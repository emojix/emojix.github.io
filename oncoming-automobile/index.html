🚘 Oncoming Automobile

A car, being shown from the front. The windscreen and hood (bonnet in British English) of the car can be seen. facing forward.

This car shows in blue on most platforms exception Android (orange), Windows (gray), and Samsung + LG (red).

Oncoming Automobile was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
