📭 Open Mailbox With Lowered Flag

An open mailbox, with the flag resting.

The lowered flag on the side of the mailbox is used in the USA to indicate that no mail has been delivered, and that there is no mail inside to be picked up by the postal worker.

Open Mailbox With Lowered Flag was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
