🎉 Party Popper

A colorful party popper, used for party or other celebration. Appears as a conical party hat, with streamers or confetti bursting out of one end.

Design may feature stripes.

Party Popper was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
