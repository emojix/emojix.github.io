🍗 Poultry Leg

A cooked leg of turkey, chicken, or another form of poultry. This poultry leg is eaten either as part of a roast dinner, or off the bone as a fast food item.

Commonly used around Thanksgiving and Christmas time.

Poultry Leg was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
