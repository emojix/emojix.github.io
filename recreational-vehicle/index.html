🚙 Sport Utility Vehicle

A vehicle used for recreation, which is generally understood to mean a campervan or motorhome.

May have originally been intended to represent an SUV, based on most versions of this emoji featuring a large vehicle with a spare tyre on the back.

Sport Utility Vehicle was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Recreational Vehicle” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
