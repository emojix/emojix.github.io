🤖 Robot Face

A robot face, showing a metallic head with eyes and a grill-like mouth that is similar to a grimace. Was available in Gmail prior to its inclusion in Unicode 8.

Robot Face was approved as part of Unicode 8.0 in 2015 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
