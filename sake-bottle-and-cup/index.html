🍶 Sake

An alcoholic beverage popular in Japan, sometimes called rice wine as it is made from rice. Shown as a sake bottle, and a small bowl-like cup in which sake is generally served.

Sake was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Sake Bottle and Cup” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
