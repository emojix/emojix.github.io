About Samsung Emojis

Samsung devices use their own emojis, which are separate from the flat-color Google Noto Emoji Font. These glossy emoji images appear on devices such as the Samsung Galaxy and Galaxy Note.

WhatsApp uses Apple emojis instead of Google or Samsung emojis.
