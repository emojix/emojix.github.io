⚖️ Balance Scale

Balance scales which are commonly used in reference to the scales held by Lady Justice, representing the weighing of evidence.

Balance Scale was approved as part of Unicode 4.1 in 2005 under the name “Scales” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
