🎒 School Backpack

A school bag Emoji (also called a satchel) used by children attending school to carry books, lunch, and other personal belongings.

School Backpack was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “School Satchel” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
