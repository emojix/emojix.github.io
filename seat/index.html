💺 Seat

A seat, which could refer to any form of place to sit, with the distinction that a seat is generally fixed, and is not a chair (which can be moved).

Due to the location of this emoji amongst other transport related characters, it is presumed this is intended to be a seat on some form of mass-transit, such as on an airplane, train or bus.

Most platforms display this looking similar to an airplane seat, except Google which shows an orange wooden chair.

Seat was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
