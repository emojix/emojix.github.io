🍕 Pizza

A slice  of pizza, with pepperoni topping on it. The Android pizza emoji includes  a slice of green pepper / capsicum as well. How exotic!

Pizza was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Slice of Pizza” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
