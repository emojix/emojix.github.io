😅 Smiling Face With Open Mouth & Cold Sweat

The same face as Smiling Face With Open Mouth And Smiling Eyes, but with a single drop of sweat on one side of the face.

A rarely used emoji, as few people look this happy while sweating.

Smiling Face With Open Mouth & Cold Sweat was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Smiling Face With Open Mouth and Cold Sweat” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
