💦 Sweat Droplets

Beads of sweat splashing in a single direction. Is not distinguishable from splashing water, and is often used as water instead of sweat.

Sometimes referred to as plewds when used in comic books or animations showing a character is working hard, or stressed.

Sweat Droplets was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Splashing Sweat Symbol” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
