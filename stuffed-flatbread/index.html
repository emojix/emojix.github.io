Flatbread containing items such as salad, sliced meat, or falafel. A fast food item popular throughout parts of the middle east. May come in the form of a wrap, pita, or sandwich. Commonly referred to as the Doner Kebab emoji.

To distinguish this emoji from the burrito, this emoji mockup is displayed in bread.

Stuffed Flatbread was approved as part of Unicode 9.0 in 2016 and added to Emoji 3.0 in 2016.
