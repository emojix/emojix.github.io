🎾 Tennis

A tennis racket (racquet) with a tennis ball. Only a ball is shown on Apple, LG, Twitter, Facebook, and Mozilla platforms.

Tennis was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Tennis Racquet and Ball” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
