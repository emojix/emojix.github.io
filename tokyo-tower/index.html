The Tokyo Tower is the second-tallest building in Japan, located in Minato, Tokyo.

Built in 1957, the design of the Tokyo Tower  was inspired by the Eiffel Tower in Paris, France and may be confused for it due to the small size of the emoji.

Tokyo Tower was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
