🚊 Tram

A form of public transport on rails, usually found on streets in cities or other urban areas. For short or medium-length journeys.

Trams tend to be similar to light rail, with the difference being that they don’t tend to have their own dedicated tracks.

This emoji is generally shown as the front of the tram, on tracks. The intended usage of this emoji, instead of the tram car emoji is not clear.

Tram was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
