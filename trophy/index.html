🏆 Trophy

A trophy given as an recognition of achievement, usually in sports. The trophy emoji is a gold cup, with a engraving on the bottom detailing the award.

In Snapchat, the trophy provides access to the Trophy Case for unlocking emoji achievements.

Trophy was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
