🐠 Tropical Fish

A fish that lives in tropical (warm) water, generally colorful in appearance.

Shown as a fish with a blue top and yellow bottom half in Apple’s artwork, and as an orange clownfish in Google’s.

Tropical Fish was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
