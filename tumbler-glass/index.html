🥃 Tumbler Glass

A flat-bottomed glass, often associated with drinks such as whisky or rum. Shown with ice cubes and liquid inside the glass.

Tumbler Glass was approved as part of Unicode 9.0 in 2016 and added to Emoji 3.0 in 2016.
