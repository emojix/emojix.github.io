🔀 Shuffle Tracks Button

Twisted arrows pointing right. Used to indicate putting music on ‘shuffle’, randomizing the order of tracks in the upcoming playlist.

Shuffle Tracks Button was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Twisted Rightwards Arrows” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
