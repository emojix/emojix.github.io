🔼 Up Button

A triangle-shaped arrow, pointing up. Styled similarly to the left and right pointing triangles on iOS, but shown in red on Windows, and orange on Android.

Up Button was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Up-Pointing Small Red Triangle” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
