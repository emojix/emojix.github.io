A wave in the ocean or open sea. Could be used to surf on, or to jump waves at the beach.

The Apple artwork for this emoji is based on the famous 1830s Japanese woodblock print: The Great Wave off Kanazawa.

Water Wave was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
