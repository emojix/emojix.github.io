🌔 Waxing Gibbous Moon

The moon shown mostly lit, with a small portion of the left side in shadow.

Waxing Gibbous Moon was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Waxing Gibbous Moon Symbol” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
