👒 Woman’s Hat

A hat worn by women for sun protection, or for fashion.

The Apple version of this hat is made of straw and has a green bow on the front. Google shows a yellow hat on Android (with a ribbon in the latest version), while Windows and Twitter both feature a flower on the front of the hat.

Woman’s Hat was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Womans Hat” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
