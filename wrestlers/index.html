🤼 People Wrestling

Two people in the sport of wrestling.

People Wrestling was approved as part of Unicode 9.0 in 2016 under the name “Wrestlers” and added to Emoji 3.0 in 2016.

Modifiers

The Wrestlers emoji supports skin tone modifiers. A yellow (or other non-human) skin tone should be shown by default, unless an emoji modifier is applied.

  • 🤼🏽 Wrestlers, Type-4
  • 🤼🏾 Wrestlers, Type-5
